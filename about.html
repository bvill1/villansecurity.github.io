About Me

I'm Brian Villanueva, a cybersecurity strategist and blue team lead with over 15 years of experience across cyber, physical security, and emergency response. My career has spanned hands-on incident response, threat hunting, infrastructure defense, and leading convergence efforts across security domains.

I hold a master’s degree in cybersecurity and am pursuing a PhD in Information Security. I also maintain certifications such as CISSP, CISM, CASP+, CRTO (in progress), and more. My work focuses on securing critical infrastructure, building resilient detection systems, and using red team insights to inform defense strategies.

Before cybersecurity, I served in physical security and EMS/firefighting roles. That real-world urgency shapes how I think about digital risk—always focused on reliability, response, and mission continuity.

This site highlights my work, learning, and projects across the spectrum of cybersecurity operations. Feel free to explore and connect.
