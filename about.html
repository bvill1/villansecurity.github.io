About Me

I'm Brian Villanueva, a cybersecurity strategist and blue team lead with 15+ years of experience across cyber, physical security, and emergency response. My journey includes protecting critical infrastructure, leading threat detection initiatives, and bridging the gap between offensive testing and defensive architecture.

My certifications include CISSP, CISM, CASP+, and CRTO (in progress). I hold a master’s degree in cybersecurity, and I’m currently pursuing a PhD in Information Security, with a research focus on AI applications in infrastructure defense.

With a background in firefighting, EMS, and physical security, I approach cyber defense through the lens of real-world risk, urgency, and mission continuity. I build defenses with purpose—and teach others to do the same.
