Professional Background

My career spans emergency services, physical security, and cybersecurity—driven by adaptability and real-world mission focus. Starting as a certified Illinois Firefighter and EMT, I thrived in high-pressure environments, mastering critical thinking and decision-making when lives were on the line. As a Basic Life Support Instructor, I empowered others by teaching life-saving skills, and in physical security management, I developed and implemented policies that enhanced safety and operational resilience.

Transitioning to cybersecurity, I have combined my multidisciplinary background with technical expertise to address evolving security challenges. My certifications and on-going education reflect my commitment to ongoing learning and excellence. Actively engaged in practical labs like TryHackMe and HackTheBox, I specialize in incident response, ethical hacking, and cyberdefense strategies.

Vision and Aspirations

With a future-focused mindset, I aim to shape security at the intersection of people, policy, and technology. Currently pursuing a PhD in Information Technology with a focus on AI and Information Security, I am passionate about merging cutting-edge research with hands-on experience. My vision is to advance security programs that bridge the gap between cybersecurity and physical security, ensuring a holistic approach to organizational defense. By leveraging AI and emerging technologies, I aim to tackle tomorrow’s threats while building resilient systems that adapt to today’s complex environments.
