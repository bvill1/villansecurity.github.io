Defense with Purpose

Cybersecurity isn’t just about firewalls and logs — it’s about protecting people, systems, and mission-critical processes from real harm. My background in emergency response taught me that clarity, speed, and trust are essential under pressure. I bring that same mindset into every defensive engagement I lead.

My Areas of Focus

  • Phishing Defense: I investigate dozens of phishing attempts daily, validate payloads, and tune detections. I run end-user simulations, improve reporting culture, and build awareness campaigns grounded in real-world attacks.
  • Email Security: I manage the full stack — from secure email gateways to M365 hardening and mail flow rules. I design layered defenses with DMARC, SPF, DKIM, impersonation controls, and anomaly-based detection.
  • Incident Response: I lead alert triage, coordinate across teams, and guide the response cycle from identification through containment and lessons learned. Whether it's endpoint compromise or insider threats, I respond with clarity and control.
  • Security Engineering: I actively collaborate on infrastructure projects, infusing security into AD hardening, virtual environments, and Microsoft cloud services. I validate technical controls and audit them against emerging risks.

How I Work

I believe defenders should think like attackers — not to break things, but to build them stronger. I study offensive tactics so I can anticipate them. I test detections in lab environments. I believe in purple team collaboration, not siloed teams.

My job isn’t just to block threats — it’s to understand them, communicate risk clearly, and help the business make smarter security decisions. I don’t just chase alerts; I tune out the noise, dig into root causes, and leave each system better than I found it.

Tools in My Kit

  • SIEM: Alert tuning, custom rule creation, and correlation analysis
  • EDR/XDR: Investigation, containment, response workflow design
  • M365 Defender Suite: Email, endpoint, identity, and cloud app protection
  • Phishing Simulation: Custom campaigns and targeted user training
  • Threat Hunting: IOC correlation, pattern recognition, and proactive sweeps

Always Improving

Every phishing ticket, every false positive, every gap I uncover is an opportunity to learn. I’m constantly refining—not just my tooling, but my approach. I participate in CTFs, test detections in TryHackMe and HackTheBox labs, and study AI's role in defense as part of my doctoral work.

Ultimately, Blue Teaming isn’t just my specialty — it’s my mindset. It’s how I think, how I train, and how I lead. I’m not chasing headlines. I’m building resilient, informed defenses that keep organizations and people safe when it matters most.
