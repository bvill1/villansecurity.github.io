vCISO Services

Fractional security leadership for organizations that need clarity, prioritization, and defensible decisions.

Virtual CISO, built for small teams

Villan Security provides vCISO leadership to help you run a practical security program, communicate risk to leadership, and build a roadmap that survives audits, incidents, and budget pressure.

If you have tools and good people but no consistent governance, this is for you.

What you get

  • Program ownership with a clear operating model: decisions, prioritization, and accountability.
  • Risk visibility with board-ready reporting that stays grounded in evidence and business impact.
  • Roadmap execution support that translates policy and frameworks into usable work.
  • Vendor and tool rationalization to reduce waste and clarify what is actually delivering value.
  • Incident readiness through tabletop exercises, escalation paths, and minimum viable response planning.

Engagement model

You get a consistent cadence and defined outputs, not open-ended consulting.

  • Weekly working session (30 to 60 minutes) with your key stakeholders.
  • Asynchronous support via email or chat for quick decisions and review.
  • Monthly executive brief that summarizes risk, progress, and decisions needed.
  • Quarterly roadmap refresh based on changes in business, threat, and constraints.

30-day onboarding

  • Week 1: Discovery, goals, constraints, and current-state inventory.
  • Week 2: Risk register draft and control posture snapshot.
  • Week 3: Prioritized roadmap and operating cadence established.
  • Week 4: Executive brief v1 delivered, then we begin steady-state execution.

Packages

Foundation vCISO

Best for organizations that need structure and leadership cadence.

  • Monthly executive brief (risk and progress)
  • Risk register and top-priority roadmap
  • Policy and standards baseline (minimum viable set)
  • 1 working session per month

Core vCISO

Best for organizations actively executing a security roadmap.

  • Everything in Foundation
  • Weekly working session
  • Security program roadmap management
  • Vendor review, tool rationalization guidance, and security decision support
  • Incident readiness: escalation model, tabletop planning, and minimum viable IR runbook

Core Plus vCISO

Best for regulated or audit-driven organizations that need stronger evidence and governance.

  • Everything in Core
  • Control evidence strategy and audit readiness support
  • Third-party risk program starter kit (intake, scoring, minimum due diligence)
  • Security metrics design: what to measure, how to report it, and what decisions it supports

Pricing depends on scope and complexity. If we talk and the fit is wrong, I will say so.

Who this is for

  • SMBs with an IT leader but no dedicated security leadership
  • Teams under 1,000 users that need governance and a defensible security program
  • Organizations preparing for audits, insurance scrutiny, customer questionnaires, or incident response maturity

Who this is not for

  • Organizations looking for 24/7 SOC coverage or a managed detection service
  • Companies that want checkbox compliance without real risk ownership
  • Teams that need a full-time CISO on-site

Common outcomes after 90 days

  • Clear top risks, owners, and a pragmatic roadmap tied to business constraints
  • Leadership reporting that reduces panic decisions and supports prioritization
  • Policies and standards that are used, not shelfware
  • Incident response readiness that is executable under pressure

Start with a fit check

If you describe your environment, constraints, and what you are trying to achieve, I will respond with a recommended engagement level and the first 30-day plan.
