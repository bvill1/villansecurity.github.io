Security Leadership, Without the Full-Time Hire

Most growing organizations reach a point where IT and security stop being the same thing. Your IT provider handles infrastructure. Your team handles operations. But security decisions — what risks are acceptable, what controls matter, what to do when something goes wrong — fall through the cracks. That gap is where breaches happen, where cyber insurance renewals get complicated, and where leadership makes expensive decisions without the right information.
